### TYPE 5 with .json
## Pack
PackMakerLite.exe --create_cs five --add_cs_map metin2_map_ox --pack five
## Unpack
PackMakerLite.exe --load_cs five --unpack five

### TYPE 5 without .json
## Pack
PackMakerLite.exe --forcetype 5 --add_cs_map metin2_map_ox -p five
## Unpack
PackMakerLite.exe -u five
