GAME RULES[ENTER]
[ENTER]
Hunt down monsters to collect King Cards.[ENTER]
Together 25 King Cards make up a King Deck,[ENTER]
with which you can play a game of Catch the[ENTER]
King.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
[ENTER]
AIM OF THE GAME[ENTER]
[ENTER]
Reveal as many cards as possible and collect[ENTER]
points. Catch the king in one go on the very[ENTER]
last move to pick up 100 points at once. If[ENTER]
you earn at least 10 points, you can redeem a[ENTER]
prize at the end of the game.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
You can wager up to 5 King Decks each time you[ENTER]
play, earning the same number of chests in[ENTER]
reward. Please note: The number of cards you[ENTER]
play with is fixed; it is not influenced by[ENTER]
the number of decks you wager. Once the game[ENTER]
has started, you cannot reclaim the King Decks[ENTER]
you have wagered.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
[ENTER]
HOW TO PLAY[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ You always start with the lowest card. An[ENTER]
arrow indicates which card can be selected.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ By clicking on the card it will appear in[ENTER]
the 'My Card' slot. In subsequent turns, cards[ENTER]
of the same value will be moved automatically.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ Turn over any face-down card on the game[ENTER]
board and compare it to your own.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If your card is greater, you will receive[ENTER]
points equal to the value of the revealed card[ENTER]
and you can turn over another card on the[ENTER]
board.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If the cards are the same value, you receive[ENTER]
the points and your turn is over. Play[ENTER]
continues with a new card from your deck.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If your card's value is lower, you don't[ENTER]
receive any points. Your turn is over and play[ENTER]
continues with a new card.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If your card is a 5 and there is a face-down[ENTER]
5 neighbouring the card you reveal, your card[ENTER]
will be captured. Your turn is over and you[ENTER]
don't receive any points.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If your card is the king (K) and you catch[ENTER]
the king on the board in exactly one move, you[ENTER]
receive 100 points. The game ends after this[ENTER]
move.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ Bingo! If you reveal all cards in a row, you[ENTER]
receive 10 extra points.[ENTER]
[ENTER]
+ If you've earned at least 10 points, you can[ENTER]
collect your prize by clicking on 'Your[ENTER]
Reward'.[ENTER]
